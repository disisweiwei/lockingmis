Uncover hidden clues, explore locations, and crack the codes
HOW THE GAME WORKS
1
First clue appears on the website. — Your team will receive the opening riddle. Read carefully and find the correct spot.
2
Find the correct classroom / location. — Use the clue to navigate around the campus. Each location matches one unique riddle.
3
Station keeper & challenge. — At every station, a station keeper will supervise. Complete the activity or challenge they give you.
4
Receive a special code. — After finishing the station task, the keeper will give your group a unique 4-digit code.
5
Enter the code on the website. — Go back to the game page and type the code. If correct, the next clue will be revealed instantly.
6
Next clue appears → repeat. — Follow the new clue to the next station. The adventure continues until all locations are discovered.
7
Finish all stations & win! — After unlocking every location, your team completes the Scavenger Hunt. Celebrate victory! 🏆
Stay with your team. Only official station keepers provide codes. Good luck, hunters!
